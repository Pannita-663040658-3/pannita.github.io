My Blog

Learning Programing: Challenges and Solutions

January 15, 2025

I first got into coding through HTML, which sparked my interest in web design and development. I practiced regularly in class and on my own, which inspired me to learn more about becoming a web developer. However, when I entered university and started learning Python, I realized how different and challenging it was compared to HTML. At first, I struggled to understand the syntax and concepts, but with the help of tutorials, classmates, and consistent practice, I managed to improve step by step.

Later, I moved on to Java, which was even more complex and at times frustrating to learn. Despite the challenges, I found it exciting to solve more advanced problems and kept pushing myself to understand the concepts. That mindset helped me approach each new language and technology with more patience and determination.

In my Advanced Computer Programming course, I worked on a project using Flutter to develop a web application. It was my first experience with Flutter, and I faced many difficulties, from understanding its limitations to finding good resources. Still, by working with my teammates and troubleshooting problems one by one, we successfully built a fully functional app. This experience taught me the value of persistence, teamwork, and learning from challenges along the way.
