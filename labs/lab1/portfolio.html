My Blog

Learning Programing: Challenges and Solutions

January 15, 2025

Programing has been one of the most rewarding yet challenging aspects of my computer engineering studies. When I first started with Python in my freshman year, I struggled with concepts like loops, functions, and object-oriented programming. The syntax seemed overwhelming, and I often felt lost when trying to solve complex problems.

What helped me improve was consistent practice and breaking down problem into smaller, manageable pieces. I started solving simple programing challenges on platforms like HackerRank and gradually worked my way up to more complex algorithms, I also found that explaining my code to classmates help me understand concepts better.

Now, as I work with web technologies like JavaScript, I can see how the fundamental programing concepts I learned with Python apply across different languages. The key is to be patient with yourself and celebrate small victories along the way
